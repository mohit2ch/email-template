About Adore India:

ADORE INDIA motivates the youth to identify and undertake positive activities for a better society and a better India. We are a voluntary network of University students and professionals who interact with students so that they grow and flair as successful and countable professional in the arena of their career, emerge as aware and responsible citizens and better human beings. The interactive sessions are on various subjects from Cleanliness to Soft skills, from Career Orientation to Self-Development, etc. Our volunteers are located all over the country.
